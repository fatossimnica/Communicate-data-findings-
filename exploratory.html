What is the structure of your dataset?¶

This dataset has 15 columns and 183412 rows. It includes continues and categorical, Independent and dependent variables.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

The main features of this project are the duration, number of trips, days were the most trips are taken at the same time to see how the gorups of costumers compare with each other.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

This dataset has very interesting features that can help us investigate our matters. The first column that can help us is "duration_sec" from which we can extract the duration of the trip. On the other hand, "start_time" can help us find out the days where the most trips are done. The "user_type" can help us see how different groups of customers compare with each other. we would also want to know how the genders compare with each other and to help us do that is the "member_gender" column.
